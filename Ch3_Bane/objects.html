Foundations of Web Design

Objects: Images

When you want to add images to a website, you can use the <img> element. Here are some of the different formats you can use:

JPG:

Good for photographs and artwork that has gradients

PNG:

PNG-24

Good for photos and illustrations that have a transparent background

PNG-8

Format for flat-color images that don't require transparency and have no gradients. While GIFs were once used, PNG-8 gives us a smaller file size.

SVG:

Also great for icons or any illustration created in a vector applications, but these images can scale without losing quality.
